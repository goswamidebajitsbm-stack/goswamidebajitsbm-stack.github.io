Security Policy

Last updated: 2026

Our Commitment

Singularity Union prioritizes system integrity, data protection, and secure engineering practices. All infrastructure and applications are designed following standard cybersecurity principles.

Data Protection

  • HTTPS encryption enforced
  • No selling of user personal data
  • Minimal data collection principle
  • Secure storage practices

Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a vulnerability, report it to:

security@singularityunion.tech

We will investigate and respond within a reasonable time.

Third-Party Services

Some infrastructure may rely on third-party providers. Their security practices also apply when interacting with our services.
