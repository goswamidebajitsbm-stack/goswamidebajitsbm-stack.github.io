Privacy Policy

We engineer systems that respect human and corporate intelligence. At Singularity Union, data sovereignty is absolute. Read our strict parameters regarding data collection, logging, and your autonomy.

01 // Collection Parameters

Consent-Driven Architecture.

Singularity Union operates on absolute data minimization protocols. We do not autonomously harvest, scrape, or extract personal identifiers from our users.

Any intelligence, communication, or data transfer initiated by you—whether for deployment requests or general inquiries—is strictly voluntary and processed solely for the execution of its intended operational parameter.

02 // Data Transmission

Zero Data Brokering.

Your proprietary intelligence remains yours. We categorically prohibit the sale, trading, or unauthorized distribution of user profiles or corporate datasets to any third-party entities.

Client data is never utilized to train generalized global models without explicit, cryptographically verifiable consent.

03 // Security Logging

Diagnostic Telemetry.

To guarantee the deterministic reliability and security of our infrastructure, our systems may temporarily cache basic network telemetry (e.g., routing protocols, latency metrics, and error logs). These logs are ephemeral, heavily encrypted, and utilized exclusively for active threat detection and core system optimization.

04 // User Control

Data Autonomy.

You retain complete control over your digital footprint within our ecosystem. Users and organizations hold the absolute right to request full cryptographic deletion of their communication logs and associated telemetry from our active servers at any time.
